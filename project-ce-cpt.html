Creative Estuary – Cultural Planning Toolkit

Creative Estuary – Cultural Planning Toolkit

Website design & build (with Playne Design)

Creative Estuary commissioned Playne to create a website platform to host their cultural planning toolkit – a long and text-heavy document that contains a lot of critical information on applying for funding and grants in the creative sector.

This website was prototyped in InDesign as a quick visual, and fully developed entirely in Webflow. My experience with Figma and growing knowledge of web design languages helped us achieve a microsite to successfully communicate what would have been a monotonous and laborious document to read.

See this website live at creativeestuary.com
