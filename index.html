In Progress Project:

NameNest – A name sharing and voting app for new parents

Deciding on a name for a child is difficult – both parents have their likes and their dislikes, meaning that alignment isn't always easy. This app aims to store and list desired names in popularity order by offering the users a function to up or down vote their favourite names. This way, names that resonate with both parents float to the top and the disliked names are pushed to the bottom.
