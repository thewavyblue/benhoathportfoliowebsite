A little bit about me

For 12 years I’ve developed a career around graphic design for print and digital platforms. My experience is rooted in illustration and graphic design, having graduated with a BA Hons Illustration from University of Portsmouth.

Since then, my creative journey has taken me through product design, branding, identity, marketing, and advertising, working independently and as part of a team in fast-paced agency environments.

I’m looking to use my expertise to transition into a technically involved role that helps me step out of my comfort zone and encourages my career growth and development.
