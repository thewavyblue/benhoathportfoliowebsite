South Saxon Hockey Club – Brand update & social media assets

After the COVID lockdowns, SSHC was need of a social media push as their presence was pretty much non-existent. For any club to survive it needs to subscribe new players and retain its existing membership, so the idea of the identity was to keep hold of the familiar elements of the logo but push hard on the vibrant colour palette to catch the attention of fast-scrolling social media users.
