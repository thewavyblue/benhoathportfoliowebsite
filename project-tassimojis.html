Tassimojis

One day, whilst waiting for my coffee to brew whilst fiddling with a t-disc (coffee pod), I wondered what it would look like if the t-disc had a face...

A day later, I put together this short project that explores the shapes, colours and expressions that a branded emoticon system might have.

The colours where taken from TASSIMO’s brand. The shapes of the facial expressions are taken from the profile of a t-disc.
